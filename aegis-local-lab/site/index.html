Aegis Local SSL Lab

This page is served by the optional local-web container. If your custom Firefox build shows this page without a certificate warning, the trusted CA and the Aegis policy bundle both allowed the connection for localhost.

Quick checks:

  • The certificate should chain to the EJBCA Management CA.
  • With a bundle built using New-AegisPolicyBundle.ps1, the connection should succeed.
  • With -OmitRootApproval, the same hostname should be blocked by enterprise policy.
